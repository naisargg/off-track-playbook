## 2. Activation inventory by category

### A. Brand identity & livery
- HP logo + signature electric-blue accents on SF-24/SF-25 livery, race suits, team kit (2024–).
- **Special co-designed Miami GP 2025 livery** — first asymmetric graphics in Scuderia history, HP electric blue on wings, white wheels; unveiled at a downtown Miami event with Leclerc, Hamilton, and Vasseur. (HP press release; Ferrari.com)

### B. Fan-facing experiential
- **HP Experience zone, Wynwood Marketplace, Miami GP 2025** — consumer showcase framed around "work fulfillment" / future-of-work messaging. (HP press release)
- **Ferrari HP Esports Series** — HP-co-branded open fan sim-racing competition (esportsseries.ferrari.com).

### C. Programme extensions (name-in-title assets)
- **F1 Academy:** Maya Weug races in Scuderia Ferrari HP colours (2024 Yas Marina race win; P3 in 2025 standings — HP branding attached to a rising female-audience property).
- **Scuderia Ferrari HP Esports Team:** competes in F1 Sim Racing World Championship; 2026 season opener at DreamHack Birmingham (Mar 2026).

### D. Technology & product integration (the "beyond the logo" clauses)
- HP Z / ZBook workstations, EliteBook AI PCs, HP Poly collaboration tools deployed across team operations. (HP partnership page)
- **HP Large Format Printing used to produce part of the actual race livery** — engineering story HP tells publicly.
- **Co-engineering announcement (2026):** "HP Shifts into a New Gear with Ferrari, Fueling PC Innovation with Shared Design and Engineering Vision" — Ferrari design input into HP consumer PC products. This is the Snapdragon-style enterprise-integration clause being executed.

### E. Retail / commerce tie-ins
- "Scuderia Ferrari HP" branded storefront sections on HP.com shop (multiple regions), cross-selling HP hardware on team fandom.

### F. Measurement data points (public)
- One Hamilton social post ≈ **$62,100** media value to HP alone; $40K+ each for Shell, IBM, Puma et al. (Relo Metrics, Jan 2025)
- Hamilton effect estimated at **~€50M/yr** incremental sponsorship income for Ferrari. (Crash.net, industry estimate)
- F1-wide sponsor media value H1 2025: **$665M** (Relo Metrics via BlackBook Motorsport); total F1 sponsorship market **>$3B** in 2026, +15% YoY (Ampere Analysis).

## 3. Observed gaps (audit hypotheses — to verify)
1. **B2B channel activation:** no public evidence of Ferrari assets deployed at HP Amplify (HP's flagship partner conference, 2,200+ attendees) in 2025 or 2026 — either unexecuted or under-publicized. Classic "signed but not activated" candidate.
2. **Measurement narrative:** HP has published engineering stories but no public ROI framing beyond logo/media value — the measurement-gap pain point applies.
3. **Enterprise storytelling:** Poly/Z-workstation deployment is mentioned on partnership pages but thin on case-study depth vs. e.g. IBM's Ferrari app case study — IBM (a smaller partner) is out-publishing the title sponsor on proof-of-value content.
