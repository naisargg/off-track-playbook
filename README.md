# The Off-Track Playbook

Five studies in Formula 1 business operations — a capstone portfolio on how an F1
team makes money off-track. Built from publicly available information only; all
financial figures are third-party estimates unless a company disclosed them.

## The five studies

| # | Study | Function | Status |
|---|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | [Sponsorship Activation Audit & ROI Dashboard — Ferrari × HP](projects/sponsorship-roi.html) | Commercial Partnerships | **Flagship** — activation inventory live, dashboard in build |
| 2 | [Sea-Freight Transition Simulator](projects/sea-freight.html) | Logistics & Supply Chain | Stub |
| 3 | [Net Zero Progress Tracker](projects/net-zero.html) | ESG & Sustainability | Stub |
| 4 | [Merchandise & Collab Strategy Teardown](projects/merch-collabs.html) | Licensing & Merchandise | Stub |

## Structure

- `index.html` — landing page
- `projects/` — one page per study
- `research/` — source-cited research notes feeding the studies
- `assets/` — shared styles

Plain static HTML/CSS, no build step — deployable as-is to GitHub Pages or Vercel.

## Build sequence

1. Flagship (Ferrari × HP) — inventory ✅ → measurement framework ✅ → interactive dashboard → Italian executive summary
