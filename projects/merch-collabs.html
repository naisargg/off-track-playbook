Merchandise & Collab Strategy Teardown — Ferrari vs. the grid

Ferrari is the only team on the grid whose licensing arm — fashion line, theme parks, luxury goods — is a genuine standalone business. This teardown maps that portfolio against rivals' collab strategies (Cadillac × Tommy Hilfiger, Williams × UNDONE, McLaren's apparel deals) and asks what each is actually optimizing for.

Deliverables

  • A positioning map of licensing strategies across all eleven teams.
  • Three concrete collab proposals aimed at F1's new fanbase — 47% of new US fans are 18–24 and over half are female.
  • A simple demand-forecast concept for 24 trackside pop-up retail operations across 21 countries.
