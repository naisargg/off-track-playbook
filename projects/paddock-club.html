Paddock Club Exclusivity Redesign

The Paddock Club is a high-margin B2B sales floor, not a catering operation — and its most common complaint from paying sponsors is exclusivity dilution at marquee races. This study answers the complaint with a product: a guest-stratification framework plus a B2B matchmaking app concept for race weekends.

Deliverables

  • A tiering framework: access rights, capacity rules, and what each guest tier is actually there to do.
  • A matchmaking concept — sponsor executives request meetings; the system pairs them by industry fit, turning the commercial director's manual matchmaking into a scalable product.
  • Why this matters commercially: hospitality and ticketing are roughly 10% of F1 revenues, and paddock access is a core sponsorship deliverable.
