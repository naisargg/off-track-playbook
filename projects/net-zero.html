Net Zero Progress Tracker

From a 2018 baseline of 228,793 tCO₂e, F1 has published a ~35% reduction — while growing from 21 to 24 races and from 4M to 6.5M attendees. This tracker turns the published numbers into a visual scorecard by category (logistics, travel, events, facilities), projected against the Net Zero 2030 target, with a scenario slider for the remaining gap.
