1 · Why Audi is here at all

The 2026 regulations are the reason: power units with roughly a 50/50 electric-combustion split running fully sustainable fuel — the one version of F1 that maps onto Vorsprung durch Technik and Audi's electrification story. The group builds its first works F1 power unit in Germany (Audi Formula Racing GmbH, Neuburg), bought the Sauber operation for its Hinwil chassis and race infrastructure, took Qatar Investment Authority capital into the holding, and put ex-Ferrari and ex-Red Bull leadership — Mattia Binotto and Jonathan Wheatley — in charge of making it credible fast.

6 · The interview drill

Why is Audi entering F1 in 2026 specifically?
The new power unit rules — ~50% electric, 100% sustainable fuel — are the first F1 formula that matches Audi's road-car story. Entering earlier would have meant racing someone else's engine concept; 2026 lets Neuburg build the first German works F1 power unit of the modern era.
How is the project structured?
Three sites, two legal entities: Audi Formula Racing GmbH in Neuburg builds the power unit; Hinwil (the former Sauber) runs chassis, operations, and the growing commercial organization; a Bicester UK technology centre recruits engineering talent. Cost-cap and PU financial regulations are policed per entity.
Who funds and who sponsors it?
Audi (Volkswagen Group) owns the project with Qatar Investment Authority minority capital in the holding. Revolut pays an estimated ~$75M/yr as title partner — senior-team pricing for a debut — with bp as technical partner and Adidas in apparel.
What is the biggest business risk?
Competitive patience: a new works power unit historically takes seasons to mature, and the commercial stack is priced on brand promise rather than results. The build-out has to survive a slow scoreboard — which makes disciplined activation and measurement (Study 01's subject) more important, not less.
Why hire build-phase people rather than F1 veterans?
Because the department doesn't exist yet. The postings — Marketing Director "building a new department," Brand Activation, Live Experience, Live Marketing Ops — are start-up jobs inside a corporate: writing playbooks, not executing inherited ones. Transformation experience is the qualification.
